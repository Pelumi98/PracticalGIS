WHY CHOOSE US

We advance the technology offerings of smart mapping solutions providers and companies who deliver HD map content in industries including transportation, logistics, automotive, and retail. Our clients get consistent and data-rich mapping content delivered through optimized pipelines, making their maps valuable professional and personal assets for users.

Custom Mapping Solutions for industry specific use cases.

  • Transportation.

    • Enrich maps with relevant data to optimize delivery routes and facilitate convenient passenger transit

  • Fleet Management.

    • Offer better control over fleets by applying accurate maps for vehicle tracking and asset management.

  • Agriculture.

    • Increase yields and reliably predict ROI using field maps for precise monitoring of soils, nutrients, and crop varieties.

  • Hydrolgical Analysis.

    • We help in creating hydrologic models that helps in hydrological analysis. Hydrologic model is a simplification of a real-world system (e.g., surface water, soil water, wetland, groundwater, estuary) that aids in understanding, predicting, and managing water resources. Both the flow and quality of water are commonly studied using hydrologic models.

  • DATA GATHERING.

    • Keep maps in sync with real world through crowdsourcing automated sensor data using Lidar, radar, remote infrastructure, ADAS cameras, aerial imagery, and in-field analytics.

  • DATA PROCESSING AND VALIDATION.

    • Enhance map accuracy through structured, context-relavant and valid data sets, building terrain models with real-time information presented with centimeter precision.

  • MAP COMPILATION.

    • Speed up the compilation o thematic maps by aligning custom databases with proprietary or NDS formats, optimizing tile creation and reducing map sizes through simplififed polygons.

  • SYSTEMS INTEGRATION .

    • Transfer data between distributed applications within one platform in a scheduled & ongoing way or securely connect your system with external data sources, and services.
